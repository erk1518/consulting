Meet The Founder

My name is Ryan Finnerty, founder of Sentinel Training & Consulting, LLC. I bring over 25 years of combined military, federal contract, and private-sector security experience.

My background includes leadership roles overseeing large-scale security operations, regulatory compliance, and training programs across multiple jurisdictions. Additionally, I was previously trained and certified as a COR/QAE through the Defense Contract Management Agency, bringing formal experience in contract oversight, quality assurance, and regulatory compliance to consulting engagements.

My formal education includes a Bachelor’s degree in Criminal Justice, a Certificate in Legal Studies, and a Master’s degree in Intelligence Studies, complemented by multiple certificates of study through the National Institute of Justice, supporting a foundation in legal application, risk assessment, and operational analysis.

I have served in positions responsible for:

  • Managing and supervising security operations supporting federal facilities
  • Developing and implementing training and compliance programs
  • Leading teams in high-responsibility, high-accountability environments
  • Over 15-years as a Chief Range Safety Officer (CRSO) and firearms instructor experience

In addition to operational leadership, I am a certified ASP Tactical Weapons Instructor and PPCT Threat Pattern Recognition & Defensive Tactics Instructor, with experience delivering structured training programs focused on safety, legal application, and real-world effectiveness.
