Service Offerings

Our approach emphasizes regulatory compliance, practical application, and risk mitigation, ensuring our clients are not only capable, but defensible in their actions.

Training

Training is designed to meet applicable legal standards while emphasizing:

  • Firearms
    • Drawing/Re-holstering
    • Magazine changes
    • Dealing with malfunctions
    • Principles of marksmanship
  • ASP Tactical Weapons
    • Handcuffing
    • Baton
    • OC spray
    • PPCT Threat Pattern Analysis and Defensive Tactics
  • AVIRT Active Shooter Response
  • Red Cross and HSI 1st Aid/CPR/AED

Programs are available for individuals, private security personnel, and organizations requiring structured, defensible training.

Consulting

Our consulting services help organizations identify vulnerabilities, reduce liability, and improve overall security posture. We provide:

  • Security risk assessments
  • Policy and Procedure development
  • Real-world Site-specific security recommendations

All consulting is advisory in nature and focused on delivering clear, actionable solutions tailored to each client’s environment.

Security

Through our security division, we provide professional security services designed to support safety, operational continuity, and risk reduction. Services include:

  • On-site security presence
  • Event and short-term security coverage
  • Support for investigations and insurance-related site security

Our approach prioritizes professionalism, accountability, and lawful execution at all times.

Private Security Companies

Sentinel Training & Consulting supports private security companies with standards-based training, compliance alignment, and operational development. We understand the regulatory and contractual environment security companies operate in—particularly in jurisdictions requiring formal licensing, firearms qualification, and documented training records.

  • Firearms qualification and preparatory training
  • Defensive tactics and use-of-force instruction
  • Training record structuring for compliance and audit readiness
Our goal is to ensure your personnel are not only capable in the field, but properly trained, documented, and defensiblein a regulatory or legal review.

Church Security Programs

Churches face a unique challenge: maintaining a welcoming environment while addressing real-world security risks. Sentinel provides structured training and consulting to help churches develop safe, legally sound, and discreet security programs. Services include:

  • Church safety and security assessments
  • Emergency response and active threat preparedness
  • Church volunteer or staff security team development
Our approach balances safety, discretion, and legal responsibility, helping organizations protect their congregation without disrupting their mission.

State & Government Contract Support

Sentinel provides training and consulting support aligned with the expectations of state and government security contracts. With experience in contract environments requiring strict compliance, documentation, and performance standards, we assist with:

  • Contract-aligned training program development
  • Firearms qualification and documentation support
  • Compliance-focused policy review and implementation
  • Operational advisory for multi-site or multi-jurisdiction contracts
We understand the importance of meeting contract requirements while maintaining operational efficiency and accountability.
